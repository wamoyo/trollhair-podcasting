# Outreach Template

Use this as a starting point. Personalize heavily based on the deep-dive research.

---

## Email Version

**Subject**: Quick question about [their area of expertise]

Hi [First Name],

I came across your work on [specific thing they did] and was impressed by [specific detail].

I'm Costa, founder of Troll Hair - we manufacture carbon nanotubes for [relevant application to them]. I host a podcast where I talk with leaders in [their industry] about [relevant topic].

I'd love to have you on for a 30-minute conversation about [specific topic relevant to them]. No prep required on your end - just a casual conversation about your experience with [topic].

Would you be open to a quick chat to see if it's a fit?

Best,
Costa

---

## LinkedIn Version

Hi [First Name] - I saw your work on [specific thing] and thought it was fascinating.

I host a podcast focused on [relevant industry topic] and would love to have you on for a conversation about [specific angle].

Would you be open to a quick chat?

---

## Personalization Notes

Before sending, ensure you've:
- [ ] Referenced something specific from their background
- [ ] Connected it to a relevant topic for the podcast
- [ ] Made clear why THEY specifically would be a great guest
- [ ] Kept it short (under 150 words for email, under 75 for LinkedIn)
